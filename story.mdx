## Hey there!

I'm **Abhi**, the creator of **Sync UI**. This is the story of how Sync UI started, grew, and evolved over 1 year.

## How It All Started

During Navratri 2024, I realized there weren't any **animated MUI component libraries** like the Tailwind + Framer Motion ones out there. So I decided to build **Sync UI** – a library where components stay in sync with **design, motion, and developer experience**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="The Inspiration">
    I started small, building components one by one, taking inspiration from Tailwind + Framer Motion libraries but implementing everything in **MUI + Motion (motion/react)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finding the Style">
    Each component gradually gained its own **unique touches** to fit Sync UI's style and layout, creating a cohesive design system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Early Components

By the first month, I had built:

- **10 core components**
- **3-4 variations each**
- Around **30-40 ready-to-use components**

This was the foundation for Sync UI, giving developers a **plug-and-play library** of animated components they could use instantly.

<Tip>
  The focus from day one was on making components that developers could simply copy and paste into their projects - no complex setup required.
</Tip>

## Naming & Launch

After brainstorming, I chose **Sync UI** – reflecting the idea that **everything stays in sync: design, motion, and developer experience.**

The first release coincided with **Navratri 2024**, and the response from friends and fellow developers was amazing!
